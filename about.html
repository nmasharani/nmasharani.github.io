I'm a Product Manager at Google working on Pixel Camera Software. Previously at Google, I worked on Health on Google Search, Android Apps, and the Google Store. I was also part of the Associate Product Manager program at Google. Outside of work, I am a volunteer with Planned Parenthood Mar Monte as part of the Young Professionals Committee. I also teach a Product Management class to students of the Tech Exchange program. I formerly served as as member of the Computer History Museum's Nextgen Advisory Board, and have previously interned in Scott Klemmer's HCI lab at Stanford, at Amyris, and at the McManus lab at USCF. I graduated from Stanford with a BS in CS, with a focus in Theory.
